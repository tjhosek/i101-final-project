I101 Final Project Home Page

Welcome to our I101 Website! Here we will describe how to better track school attendance at events such as plays, concerts, and sporting events. We started the process by first finding ways to keep track of attendance and then deciding how to encourage more attendance at the events. The solution to our problem could be implemented in any school looking to find a better way to get students more involved while also keeping things organized.
